## **History/Info**

Sylva is a Faun trapper who has turned to capturing corrupted creatures for profit. She's more pragmatic than cruel, seeing the corruption as an opportunity to make coin from Haven's desperation. She resents Haven for their exploitation but has learned to profit from it rather than fight it.
