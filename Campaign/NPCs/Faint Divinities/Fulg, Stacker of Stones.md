## Appearance

- Squat, troll like figure made of stacking stones
- 4 ft tall 
- Mossy 'hair' and glowing pebble eyes 
- Often appears as an innocuous pile of rocks by a riverbank, only animating when approached.
- In visions or dreams, they resemble a crumbling statue, whispering through grinding stones.

## **Campaign Interactions & Quests**

### **Session 4: Recovery and Preparation**
- **Met party on journey** back to Monks Eliegh
- **Fern restored balance** by placing missing stone in cairn
- **Thanked Fern** for restoring balance
- **Spoke of careful equilibrium** and how Witherwild threw steps into chaos
- **Warned about coordinated action** becoming disorder
- **Revealed information about other faint divinities:**
  - Ikla the Sky Painter (tired of painting same spring sky)
  - Oove, the Watcher of the Night Bloom (whom Ikla favored)
  - Bolgin (long silent, now impossible to reach)
- **Admitted wandering away** from many waters due to tainted rivers
- **Offered guidance:** "Follow the cairns if you become lost"
- **Snicket was skeptical** and disbelieving
- **Gnirth insulted him** but was calmly named "no different than a flower"

### **Session 6: The Siege of Serpents Hope - Part 1**
- **Met party on trail** from Low Passage to Serpents Hope farms
- **More friendly in nature** than previous encounters
- **Introduced himself to other party members** (Sabriel, Snicket) who hadn't met him before
- **Delivered Haven intelligence report** that had been misdirected
- **Cracked open wet rock** to reveal scroll with Haven's seal
- **Exchanged pleasantries** including praise for party finding his markers
- **Wished party well** before returning to stone form

### **Current Status**
- **Location:** Wandering near Monks Eliegh

- **Available Services:**
