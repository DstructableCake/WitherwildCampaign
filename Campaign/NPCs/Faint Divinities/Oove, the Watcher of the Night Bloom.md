## Appearance

- Never fully seen—manifests as glowing eyes in darkness or a shadowy owl silhouette
- Whispers come from night blooms, which bloom brighter in their presence

## Roleplay
DM
%%

> [!NOTE]
> - Mysterious, watchful guardian
> - Plays as a cryptic mentor, speaking in riddles and echoes
> - Uses a soft, echoing whisper: "The blooms... see all..."
> - Benevolent but judgmental—tests mortals' worth before aiding, punishing harm to nocturnal life

%%
## Location

- Dark forests
- Moonlit groves
- During night-week in [[Fanewick]]
- "Home" is rumoured on the moon, accessed via bloom portals

## Requirements for Assistance
DM
%%

> [!NOTE]
> - Guides (e.g., safe night travel, secrets)
> - Requires "night tribute"—share a secret under moonlight or protect nocturnal creatures
> - Assists freely if it restores balance, but demands oaths (e.g., vow against harming night-blooms)
