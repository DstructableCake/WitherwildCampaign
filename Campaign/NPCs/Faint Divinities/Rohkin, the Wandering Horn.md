## Appearance

- Massive, ethereal stag with branching antlers like tree roots
- Fur shifts with seasons
- Appears as a distant horned shadow or in herd migrations.

## Roleplay
DM
%%

> [!NOTE]
> - Stoic, protective wanderer
> - Plays as a gruff ranger, speaking in animal metaphors
> - Uses a deep, rumbling voice: "The herd endures... do you?"
> - Loyal but tests strength—challenges players with hunts or riddles

%%
## Location

- Herds
- Plains
- Wild paths in [[Fanewick]]
- Roams, avoiding [[Haven]] but scouting borders

## Requirements for Assistance
DM
%%

> [!NOTE]
> - Aids protection/tracking (e.g., safe herd paths)
> - Demands "horn tribute"—hunt ethically or protect animals (e.g., save a Withered calf)
> - Refuses if players harm beasts
