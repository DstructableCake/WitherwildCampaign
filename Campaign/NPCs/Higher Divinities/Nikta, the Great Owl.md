## Appearance

- Dual-eyed owl-goddess: one eye blooming ([[Sowing Eye]]), one withering ([[Reaping Eye]])
- In Mortal Realm, manifests as massive owls or seasonal storms.

## Roleplay

DM Only 
%%

> [!INFO] Roleplay
> - Wise but fractured (post-maiming)
> - Uses dual voices (one hopeful, one despairing)
> - Primarily found in ancient groves or sites of great natural significance.

%%

## Requirements for Assistance

DM Only
%%
> [!INFO] Assistance
> - Bargains for balance (e.g., sacrifice growth for decay, or vice versa)
> - Assistance is granted only if it serves to restore natural cycles.

%%
## World View

DM Only
%%
> [!INFO] World View
> - Agonised by eternal spring; seeks restoration of natural cycles (growth and decay) but fears full blindness (losing her [[Sowing Eye]]).
> - Sees both [[Haven]]'s actions and the [[Fanewraith]]'s extreme solution as dangerous imbalances.
