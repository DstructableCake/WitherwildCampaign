## **History/Info**

Rorik is a hardened hunter who has turned to capturing Witherwild-corrupted creatures for profit. He sees the corruption as an opportunity rather than a tragedy, viewing the tainted creatures as valuable commodities for Haven's research labs. His arms are scarred from encounters with corrupted vines, but he's learned to use this to his advantage.
