# Quartermaster Brill

%%

## Playing Character

Human Haven commander stationed at Serpents Hope farms. Overworked, pragmatic, focused on supplies and logistics. Rumpled uniform, exhausted frown, blood-stained sleeve from treating wounded. Voice clipped, hoarse from shouting orders.

%%

## History/Info

Quartermaster Brill is the local Haven commander responsible for overseeing Serpents Hope farms operations. He manages the agricultural facility's security, supply chains, and coordination with Haven's military command. The farms are a critical resource for Haven, producing Lady's Veil flowers and other agricultural goods.

%%

## What They Want

- **Short-Term:** Maintain farm security and supply lines
- **Mid-Term:** Report accurate intelligence to Haven command
- **Long-Term:** Ensure agricultural facilities remain operational despite threats

## What They Know

- **Military Logistics:** Expert at managing supplies, guards, and farm operations
- **Threat Assessment:** Aware of coordinated attacks on agricultural facilities
- **Haven Intelligence:** Receives reports from Haven command about corruption and threats
- **PC Hooks:**
  - Can provide supplies and equipment
  - Has information about Alula routes
  - Knows about coordinated attack patterns
  - Can offer rewards for successful defense

%%

## Relations

- **Haven Command:** Reports to higher command structure
- **Farm Workers:** Oversees Wickling workers and their families
- **Party:** Grateful for warnings and defense, but burdened by reports he must send
