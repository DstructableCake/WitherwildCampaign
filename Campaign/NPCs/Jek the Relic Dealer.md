## **History/Info**

Jek is a human relic dealer who operates in the shadows of the Low Passage night market. He deals in relics, forbidden wares, and exotic items of questionable origin. He's all smiles and charm, but his eyes are sharp for cheats and his prices are steep for anything truly valuable.
