## **History/Info**

Mira is a Fungril herbalist who operates a mystical stall in the Low Passage night market. Her stall is grown into the root-wall itself, showing her deep connection to the Fungril network. She's an eccentric mystic who barters knowledge and potions, always offering cryptic warnings about the deeper mysteries of the world.
