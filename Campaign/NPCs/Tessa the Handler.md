## **History/Info**

Tessa is an Elf handler who has become hardened by loss. She lost family members to the Serpent Sickness and has rationalized her work capturing corrupted creatures as a form of mercy. She believes Haven is responsible for the corruption and sees her work as cleaning up their mess.

- **Long-Term:** Hope that Haven's research will lead to a cure
