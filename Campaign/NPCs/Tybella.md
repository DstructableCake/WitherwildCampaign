## **History/Info**

Tybella is a Fungril migrant from the Blue Bell Tribe, located deep in the southern Witherwild. She was displaced by the corruption and found refuge in Haven's barracks, where she works in the kitchens feeding the troops. She feels grateful to Haven for providing her with safety and purpose, even though she wasn't directly rescued by them.
