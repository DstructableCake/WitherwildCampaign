### [[Cackling Moor]]

- **Description:** A foggy, laughter-echoing moorland dotted with gnarled trees and bubbling tar pits. Witherwild corruption causes plants to "cackle" (rustling leaves mimic mad giggles), attracting nocturnal predators during night-week. Scattered Wickling huts offer uneasy shelter, lit by night blooms.
- **Lore Ties:** Embodies uncanny whimsy and terror; Faint Divinity Qui'Gar (presiding over thorny deaths) haunts here, demanding tributes for safe passage. Ties to underborne communities (bog survival customs) and themes of grief (lost travelers "laugh" eternally as Withered hybrids). Mechanics: Environmental hazards grant Wither tokens; Fear Die rolls for auditory illusions.
- **Map Position:** Southeastern bogs, near misty lowlands with tar pit icons.
- **Hooks/Ties to Campaign:** Fanewraith uses it as a rebel hideout—mission to infiltrate (rogue stealth, non-combat negotiation with Qui'Gar). Fanewick players investigate a "cackling" corruption source; Guardian regrets invading here. Low-stakes intro: Navigate illusions to rescue a trapped NPC, practicing Fear mechanics.

### [[Dreary Way]]

- **Description:** A winding, overgrown path through misty valleys, lined with petrified statues of early Haven invaders. Rain-slick stones and creeping vines make travel hazardous; during day-week, sun rose vines block sections, forcing detours.
- **Lore Ties:** Symbolizes cultural clash (Haven's failed incursions into Fanewick); ties to Serpent's Sickness (statues from plague victims). Wanderborne communities use it for transient camps. Themes of survival and transformation (vines "consume" unwary travelers). Mechanics: Path hazards add Wither tokens or Fear.
- **Map Position:** Central winding road, connecting forests to bogs—likely a labeled path with ruin markers.
- **Hooks/Ties to Campaign:** Kreil's informants travel it to Alula—ambush mission (optional combat with Withered bandits). Rogue spreads rumors here; Fanewick players protect it as a trade route. Tie to inciting incident: A statue "whispers" clues about Nikta's location.

### [[Fire Falls]]

- **Description:** Cascading waterfalls ignited by underground vents, creating rivers of flame that illuminate night-week. Surrounding cliffs are etched with ancient runes; Witherwild causes fiery flora (burning lady's veil patches) to thrive, drawing heat-seeking beasts.
- **Lore Ties:** Ties to infernis ancestries (horns grow faster near heat) and Faint Divinity Ikla (paints fiery sunsets here). Haven miners once quarried for resources, sparking clashes. Themes of ends justify means (Haven's exploitation) and epic heroism (natural wonder turned deadly). Mechanics: Fire hazards grant Wither tokens; hope for creative navigation.
- **Map Position:** Northeastern mountains, with waterfall and flame icons near crags.
- **Hooks/Ties to Campaign:** Mission to harvest fire-resistant crimson veil (non-combat gathering, ties to plague cure). Guardian recalls defending miners here; Fanewick players seek Ikla's boon to track Fanewraith.

### [[God's Nest]]

- **Description:** A massive, nest-like hollow in a colossal tree, home to avian Faint Divinities and their worshippers. Feathers and eggshells litter the ground; Witherwild enlarges birds to predatory sizes, with nests woven from thorny vines.
- **Lore Ties:** Central to Faint Divinities (e.g., Nikta's influence; Rohkin protects horned birds here). Wildborne communities offer tributes for blessings. Themes of people vs. nature (corrupted wildlife) and transformation (Withered avians hybridize with people). Ancestries like fauns feel connected (elongated horns mimic beaks).
- **Map Position:** Central deep forest, possibly a large tree icon elevated in the canopy.
- **Hooks/Ties to Campaign:** Nikta roosts nearby—inciting incident hook to scout for her Sowing Eye (non-violent tribute puzzle). Low-stakes: Protect eggs from Withered poachers, introducing combat basics.

### [[Keeners Pike]]

- **Description:** A jagged mountain peak impaled by ancient, spear-like crystals, used as a Wickling lookout. Winds howl like keening cries; Witherwild causes crystal vines to grow, refracting light into blinding patterns during day-week.
- **Lore Ties:** Named for a legendary ridgeborne scout ("Keener") who spotted Haven's invasion. Ties to survival themes and classes like rangers (scouting vantage). Faint Divinity Oove whispers guidance from the heights. Mechanics: High falls risk Wither tokens; Fear for disorienting lights.
- **Map Position:** Eastern mountains, a tall peak icon with crystal markers.
- **Hooks/Ties to Campaign:** Guardian's border post—mission to signal allies (non-combat signaling puzzle). Rogue spies on Haven movements; Fanewick players use it to spot Fanewraith's trail.

### [[Low Passage]]

- **Description:** A narrow, flooded tunnel system under boggy hills, used for smuggling. Bioluminescent fungi light the way; Witherwild floods it with strangling roots and oversized amphibians.
- **Lore Ties:** Slyborne rebels (like Fanewraith) use it for insurgent ops, tying to cultural clash. Seaborne/ribbet ancestries navigate it safely. Themes of vulnerability (claustrophobic darkness) and change (corrupted waterways). Mechanics: Submersion hazards add Wither tokens.
- **Map Position:** Southern lowlands, tunnel icons connecting bogs to coasts.
- **Hooks/Ties to Campaign:** Rogue-led smuggling mission to evade Haven patrols (stealth focus). Tie to inciting incident: Passage leads to Fanewraith's base.

### [[Qavviks Tower]]
