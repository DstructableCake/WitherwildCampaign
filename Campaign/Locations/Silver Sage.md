### **Location Tier:** Tier 2

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** A serene grove of silver-leaved trees, home to Wickling historians with razor-sharp defenses.
- **Sensory Details:** Metallic leaf rustles; fresh, herbal scent; cool shade with whispering winds.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Tree-ringed clearing with sage tree "library" carvings.
- **Notable Features:** Whispering central tree; Witherwild silver razors.
- **Atmosphere:** Peaceful yet guarded, blending wisdom's calm with defensive whimsy.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Witherwild sharpened leaves, turning it defensive; Haven seeks secrets.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Loreborne hub for knowledge trading.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Sage Accord" (15 years ago), establishing trade pacts with divinities.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Small (80-120 scholars).
- **Ancestries:** Elves and humans (adapted with keen senses).
- **Communities:** Loreborne (historians).
- **Age Profile:** Elder-dominant, mentoring youth.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Bards and wizards as lorekeepers.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Fulg with stone stacks; customs include "sage whispers" for wisdom.

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** Sage Council of elders.
- **Informal Power:** Master storytellers.
- **Power Dynamics:** Debates over sharing knowledge with outsiders.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Nikta's Wardens (protecting lore); neutral lore circles.
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Haven informants.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Wardens guard against informants; circles neutral.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Elder Quill (Elf, Loreborne, Bard/Leader): Wise, protective.
    - Thorne the Scribe (Human, Loreborne, Wizard/Researcher): Curious, conflicted.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Elder Quill: Divinity pacts. Reveals via knowledge trade.
    - Thorne: Fanewraith rumors. Shares after debate.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Tolerated for anti-Haven lore; base in hidden groves.
- **Rebel Activity:** Researching eyes; recruitment. Seen as knowledge allies.
- **Information Gathering:** Carved tree messages; sage whispers; scribe notes.
