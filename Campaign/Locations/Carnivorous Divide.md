### **Location Tier:** Tier 4

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** A deep canyon bridged by flesh-eating plants, enlarged into maws by Witherwild.
- **Sensory Details:** Chomping plant sounds; acidic, floral stench; treacherous winds and swaying vines.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Vast rift with vine bridges; natural chasms and plant "nests."
- **Notable Features:** Giant maw plants; unstable crossings.
- **Atmosphere:** Thrilling and uncanny, nature's dominance in a perilous divide.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Witherwild enlarged plants, blocking travel; rebels use for ambushes.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Wildborne crossing point with survival rituals.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Maw Crossing" (3 years ago), where plants consumed a Haven battalion.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Transient (20-50 travelers and guardians).
- **Ancestries:** Firbolgs and fauns (horns for sensing plants).
- **Communities:** Wildborne (survival experts).
- **Age Profile:** Adult adventurers; few settle due to dangers.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Rangers as bridge keepers.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Qui'Gar with thorny offerings; customs include "vine pacts."

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** Bridge Warden overseeing crossings.
- **Informal Power:** Plant shamans.
- **Power Dynamics:** Tensions over controlling or destroying plants.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Nikta's Wardens (guarding nature); Fanewraith's Insurgents (ambush sites).
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Withered Covenant plant-worshippers.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Wardens clash with Covenant; Insurgents use both.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Warden Vex (Firbolg, Wildborne, Ranger/Leader): Vigilant, nature-bound.
    - Thorn (Faun, Wildborne, Druid/Shaman): Eccentric, plant-whisperer.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Warden Vex: Crossing secrets. Reveals via escort quest.
    - Thorn: Qui'Gar rituals. Shares after pact.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Hidden in canyon nests; uses for traps.
- **Rebel Activity:** Ambushing travelers; recruitment. Viewed as defenders.
- **Information Gathering:** Plant "chomps" as codes; vine messages; survivor tales.

### Nikta Connection/ Link to the Greater Struggles

- **Place in the World:** Central Fanewick rift, dividing regions and symbolizing nature vs. invasion.
