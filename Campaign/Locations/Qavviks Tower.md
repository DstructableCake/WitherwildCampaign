### **Location Tier:** Tier 3

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** A crumbling stone spire overgrown with Witherwild ivy, holding ancient tomes on deicide.
- **Sensory Details:** Dust and old paper scent; flickering arcane wards humming; creaking stone and vine rustles.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Tall tower with spiraling stairs; ruined libraries and warded vaults.
- **Notable Features:** Flickering wards; ivy that "whispers" forgotten spells.
- **Atmosphere:** Uncanny and scholarly, blending whimsy of magic with terror of decay.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Witherwild ivy has breached wards, corrupting tomes; Haven uses as a meeting point.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Wizarding outpost studying Shun’Aush; traditions of lore trading.
- **Key Historical Events:** Qavvik's Fall (20 years ago), where the founder succumbed to early Sickness.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Small (50-80 scholars and guards).
- **Ancestries:** Humans and clanks (stone constructs resistant to ivy).
- **Communities:** Loreborne (knowledge keepers); some highborne exiles.
- **Age Profile:** Elder scholars dominant.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Wizards and sorcerers as researchers.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Ikla with painted wards; customs include "tome offerings" for inspiration.

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** Archivist overseeing studies.
- **Informal Power:** Secretive lore circles.
- **Power Dynamics:** Tensions between Haven loyalists and Fanewick sympathizers.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Haven's Vanguard (guarding tomes); Loreborne neutrals.
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Rebel spies seeking deicide secrets.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Vanguard protects from spies; lore circles neutral but leaky.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Archivist Qira (Human, Loreborne, Wizard/Leader): Erudite, secretive.
    - Gearheart (Clank, Highborne, Warrior/Guard): Loyal, mechanical guardian.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Qira: Deicide tomes on eyes. Reveals via research quest.
    - Gearheart: Haven vault intel. Shares if repaired or bribed.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Actively hunted; spies infiltrate for tomes.
- **Rebel Activity:** Stealing lore; sabotage. Viewed as thieves by locals.
- **Information Gathering:** Whispering ivy clues; stolen pages; spy confessions.
