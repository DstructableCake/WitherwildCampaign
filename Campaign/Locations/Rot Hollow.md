### **Location Tier:** Tier 3

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** A sunken valley of decaying vegetation, with carnivorous sludge pits and towering fungi.
- **Sensory Details:** Putrid rot stench; squelching mud sounds; humid, sticky air; unstable, sinking ground.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Natural depression with fungal "towers" as shelters; pit-riddled terrain.
- **Notable Features:** Enlarged fungril communities; Witherwild-accelerated rot.
- **Atmosphere:** Grim and transformative, where decay breeds both horror and strange beauty.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Witherwild has sped rot, creating pits; fungril adapt but grow unnaturally.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Foraging haven with fungal harvest traditions.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Spore Bloom" (5 years ago), where early corruption enlarged fungi, displacing non-fungril.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Medium (150-250 in fungal villages).
- **Ancestries:** Fungril (enlarged by Witherwild); some galapa.
- **Communities:** Underborne (bog adapters); wildborne foragers.
- **Age Profile:** Balanced, with "sporelings" (youth) thriving in fungi.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Druids as healers; sorcerers using fungal magic.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Hyacynis with blossom tributes; customs include "rot feasts" for growth.

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** Fungal Elder circle.
- **Informal Power:** Mycelium shamans predicting rot.
- **Power Dynamics:** Debates over embracing vs. fighting corruption.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Withered Covenant (embracing decay); Nikta's Wardens (seeking cure).
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Haven researchers studying rot-dust.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Covenant rivals Wardens; researchers neutral but spied on.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Elder Spore (Fungril, Underborne, Druid/Leader): Wise, enlarged guardian.
    - Mira Veilweaver (Fungril, Wildborne, Sorcerer/Healer): Compassionate, fearing corruption.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Elder Spore: Hyacynis rituals. Reveals via feast invitation.
    - Mira: Cure recipes. Shares after aid quest.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Tolerated in hidden pits; uses for recruitment.
- **Rebel Activity:** Testing corruption weapons; gathering. Seen as disruptors.
- **Information Gathering:** Spore messages; pit witnesses; fungal signs.
