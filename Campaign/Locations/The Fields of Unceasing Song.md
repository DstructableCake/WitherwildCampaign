### **Location Tier:** Tier 1

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** Meadows where wind through flowers creates melodies, corrupted into siren lures by Witherwild.
- **Sensory Details:** Haunting floral tunes; sweet pollen scent; soft, swaying grass with hidden snares.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Open fields with flower patches; scattered Wickling gazebos.
- **Notable Features:** Siren-like blooms attracting beasts; melodic wind patterns.
- **Atmosphere:** Whimsical and alluring, masking thrilling peril.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Corruption turned songs deadly, luring beasts; Haven farms nearby for veils.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Bardic gathering spot with song festivals.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Siren Bloom" (3 years ago), where lures caused a beast stampede.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Small (60-100 herders and bards).
- **Ancestries:** Fauns and humans (ears attuned to songs).
- **Communities:** Wildborne (meadow dwellers).
- **Age Profile:** Youthful bards; elders as songkeepers.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Bards as performers; rangers for beast control.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Hyacynis with melody tributes; customs include "song circles."

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** Songmaster leading festivals.
- **Informal Power:** Melody shamans.
- **Power Dynamics:** Tensions between performers and those fearing corruption.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Veil Harvesters (flower gathering); Nikta's Wardens (studying lures).
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Beast-lured cult.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Harvesters ally with Wardens; cults isolated.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Songmaster Aria (Faun, Wildborne, Bard/Leader): Melodic, optimistic.
    - Reed (Human, Wildborne, Ranger/Herder): Wary, beast-wise.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Songmaster Aria: Song lore on Nikta. Reveals via performance.
    - Reed: Lure patterns. Shares after hunt.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Uses fields for covert meetings; tolerated.
- **Rebel Activity:** Using lures for traps; recruitment via songs. Viewed as clever.
- **Information Gathering:** Melodic codes; flower whispers; bard rumors.
