### **Location Tier:** Tier 2

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** A natural crystal arch over a river, refracting rainbows but cracked by Witherwild vines.
- **Sensory Details:** Shimmering light prisms; crystalline chimes; slippery, vibrating surface underfoot.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Arched bridge with natural facets; riverbanks with vine overgrowth.
- **Notable Features:** Falling crystal shards; rainbow illusions hiding dangers.
- **Atmosphere:** Whimsical and beautiful, subverted by terrifying instability.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Vines cracked the bridge, causing collapses; wanderborne defend it from looters.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Trade landmark with crystal-gathering festivals.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Prism Fracture" (2 years ago), where corruption caused a partial collapse, isolating communities.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Transient (40-80 traders).
- **Ancestries:** Mixed wanderborne; some infernis (heat-resistant to crystal glow).
- **Communities:** Wanderborne (nomads).
- **Age Profile:** Balanced transients.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Rogues as bridge runners; bards for festivals.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Ikla with light paintings; customs include "prism dances."

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** Bridge Keeper elected by travelers.
- **Informal Power:** Crystal merchants.
- **Power Dynamics:** Debates over repairing vs. abandoning the bridge.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Veil Harvesters (trade control); Fanewraith's Insurgents (ambush point).
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Shard scavengers.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Harvesters rival Insurgents for access; scavengers opportunistic.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Keeper Lume (Infernis, Wanderborne, Rogue/Leader): Charismatic, adaptive.
    - Prism (Human, Wanderborne, Bard/Merchant): Artistic, rumor-monger.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Keeper Lume: Bridge repair lore. Reveals via trade.
    - Prism: Ikla visions. Shares after performance.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Uses for ambushes; hidden on banks.
- **Rebel Activity:** Disrupting Haven crossings; intel gathering. Viewed as protectors.
- **Information Gathering:** Refracted light signals; shard messages; traveler gossip.
