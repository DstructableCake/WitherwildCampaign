### **Location Tier:** Tier 1

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** A winding, overgrown path through misty valleys, lined with petrified statues of failed invaders.
- **Sensory Details:** Damp mist carrying a metallic tang; creaking vines and distant howls; slick, uneven stones underfoot; chill that seeps into bones.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Narrow, snaking trail with natural barriers like vine walls; scattered ruins of old Wickling waystations.
- **Notable Features:** Petrified statues whispering faint echoes; sun rose vines blocking sections during day-week.
- **Atmosphere:** Melancholic and foreboding, a path of forgotten grief where beauty (rainbow mists) hides peril.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Witherwild vines have overgrown the path, turning it into a hazard; Haven uses it for supply lines, clashing with rebels.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** A vital trade route for wanderborne, with traditions of statue-carving to honor the lost.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Statue Stand" (4 years ago), where early invaders petrified en masse, marking Haven's first major setback.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Transient (20-50 travelers/camps at a time).
- **Ancestries:** Mixed wanderborne humans and fauns (horns elongated by Witherwild for sensing vines).
- **Communities:** Wanderborne (nomadic traders); some slyborne scouts.
- **Age Profile:** Balanced, but elders share oral histories of safer times.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Rogues as scouts; merchants and caravanners.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Oove venerated with night bloom lanterns for guidance; customs include "whisper walks" to avoid misfortune.

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** No fixed governance; transient "caravan masters" lead groups.
- **Informal Power:** Storytellers who control rumors and paths.
- **Power Dynamics:** Tensions between Fanewick loyalists and Haven sympathizers over route control.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Veil Harvesters (trading along the path); Fanewraith's Insurgents (ambush points).
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Exiled Haven defectors hiding in camps.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Harvesters neutral but trade with both; Insurgents rival defectors.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Mira the Wanderer (Faun, Wanderborne, Rogue/Scout): Cautious optimist seeking lost family.
    - Old Garrick (Human, Loreborne, Bard/Storyteller): Gruff elder with tales of the past.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Mira: Knows safe detours and Fanewraith rumors. Reveals via shared travel or social roll.
    - Old Garrick: Lore on statue whispers and eye theft. Shares after a story exchange.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Actively hunted by Haven; uses hidden camps as waystations.
- **Rebel Activity:** Ambushing supplies; spreading rumors. Locals see it as protection but risky.
- **Information Gathering:** Statue "whispers" clues; coded trail markers; traveler accounts.

- **Hook 1: Whispered Secrets**
    - **Hook Discovery Methods:** Statue whisper during exploration; Garrick's tale.
    - **Quest Style:** Zelda puzzle with grief theme: Decipher statues' echoes.
    - **Quest Summary:** Uncover a hidden message about Nikta.
    - **Milestone Markers:** Investigate statues; solve riddle; confront vision.
    - **Mechanics Focus:** Puzzles, Fear Die.
    - **Impacts:** Allies with Harvesters; reveals map change; reputation as lore-seekers; reward: Echo amulet.
- **Hook 2: Vine Ambush**
    - **Hook Discovery Methods:** Random event (vine trap); Mira's plea.
    - **Quest Style:** Witcher moral choice: Aid rebels or caravan.
    - **Quest Summary:** Resolve an ambush on the path.
    - **Milestone Markers:** Scout; negotiate; resolve.
    - **Mechanics Focus:** Social rolls, combat optional.
    - **Impacts:** Faction shift; clears vines; varying reputation; reward: Trade goods.
- **Hook 3: Lost Caravan**

    - **Milestone Markers:** Track; cure or mercy; escape.
