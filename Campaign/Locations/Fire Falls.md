### **Location Tier:** Tier 3

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** Cascading waterfalls ignited by vents, forming flaming rivers amid rune-etched cliffs.
- **Sensory Details:** Roaring flames and steam hisses; acrid smoke and heat waves; scorching air with mineral tang.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Cliffside paths and natural ledges; ancient rune-carved caves.
- **Notable Features:** Fiery flora like burning lady's veil; heat-seeking Withered beasts.
- **Atmosphere:** Epic and thrilling, where natural wonder meets destructive fury.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Witherwild has fueled fiery overgrowth, displacing miners; Haven exploits for resources, clashing with locals.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Mining hub with infernis communities; traditions of sunset paintings to honor Ikla.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Flame Incursion" (2 years ago), where Haven quarrying awakened Withered beasts.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Medium (200-300 in cliff villages).
- **Ancestries:** Infernis and drakona (horns grow faster near heat).
- **Communities:** Ridgeborne (cliff dwellers); some highborne exiles.
- **Age Profile:** Adult-heavy; elders revered for mining lore.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Warriors as guards; wizards studying runes.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Ikla worshipped with artistic tributes; customs include "flame dances" for boons.

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** Council of ridgeborne elders.
- **Informal Power:** Mining guilds with economic sway.
- **Power Dynamics:** Clashes between traditionalists and Haven collaborators.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Haven's Vanguard (resource extraction); Nikta's Wardens (protecting sites).
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Infernis cult embracing fire corruption.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Vanguard rivals Wardens; cults ally opportunistically.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Kael the Flamekeeper (Infernis, Ridgeborne, Wizard/Miner): Ambitious, heat-resistant scholar.
    - Lira Sunset (Drakona, Loreborne, Bard/Artist): Creative, grieving lost art.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Kael: Rune lore on deicide. Reveals via quest or bribe.
    - Lira: Ikla's visions of Nikta. Shares after artistic exchange.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Hidden in rune caves; tolerated by anti-Haven locals.
- **Rebel Activity:** Sabotaging mines; recruiting miners. Seen as liberators.
- **Information Gathering:** Burnt messages in flames; overheard in caves; rune clues.
