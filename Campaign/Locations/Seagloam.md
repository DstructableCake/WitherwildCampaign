### **Location Tier:** Tier 1

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** A coastal village of glowing kelp huts, where night blooms wash ashore amid corrupted sea life.
- **Sensory Details:** Salty breeze with bioluminescent glow; crashing waves and pearl chimes; cool, misty air.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Huts woven from kelp; docks and tidal pools.
- **Notable Features:** Glowing pearls; Witherwild tentacled horrors in tides.
- **Atmosphere:** Whimsical and serene, contrasting terror of corrupted waters.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Witherwild corrupted sea life, disrupting trade; rebels use as escape route.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Fishing community with pearl-diving traditions.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Gloam Surge" (2 years ago), where corrupted tides flooded huts.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Small (100-150 fisherfolk).
- **Ancestries:** Humans and ribbets (adapted for diving).
- **Communities:** Seaborne (coastal traders).
- **Age Profile:** Balanced, with youth learning sails.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Rangers as fishers; rogues for pearl diving.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Oove with bloom lanterns; customs include "tide songs."

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** Harbor Master coordinating trade.
- **Informal Power:** Pearl divers with economic influence.
- **Power Dynamics:** Tensions between isolationists and rebel helpers.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Veil Harvesters (pearl trade); Fanewraith's Insurgents (escape ops).
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Corrupted sea cult.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Harvesters aid Insurgents for profit; cults isolated.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Harbor Master Kai (Ribbett, Seaborne, Ranger/Leader): Practical, tide-wise.
    - Pearl (Human, Seaborne, Rogue/Diver): Adventurous, lost companion.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Kai: Tide routes to rebels. Reveals via trade deal.
    - Pearl: Oove's sea whispers. Shares after dive quest.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Tolerated; base in hidden coves.
- **Rebel Activity:** Smuggling by sea; recruitment. Seen as protectors.
- **Information Gathering:** Glow messages; diver tales; washed-up clues.
