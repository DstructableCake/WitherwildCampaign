### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** A spiky mountain range resembling dragon tails, with thorny "tails" grown by Witherwild on rocks.
- **Sensory Details:** Howling winds through spikes; earthy, metallic scent from stone; jagged, precarious footing with vertigo drops.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Natural crags with ledge paths; ancient drakona carvings in caves.
- **Notable Features:** Thorny rock formations that "grab" intruders; vantage points for spotting Nikta.
- **Atmosphere:** Heroic and adventurous, where epic climbs meet thrilling dangers.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Witherwild thorns have made climbs deadly; used by scouts to track Nikta amid invasion.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Drakona ancestral grounds with hunting traditions.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Tail Awakening" (4 years ago), where corruption animated rocks, killing early climbers.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Small (50-100 nomads and scouts).
- **Ancestries:** Drakona (horns grow faster, aiding climbing).
- **Communities:** Ridgeborne (crag dwellers).
- **Age Profile:** Adult hunters; elders share legends.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Warriors and rangers as climbers.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Rohkin with horn tributes; customs include "spike ascents" for blessings.

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** Clan Chieftain leading hunts.
- **Informal Power:** Horn shamans interpreting signs.
- **Power Dynamics:** Clashes between traditional hunters and those allying with rebels.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Nikta's Wardens (protecting wildlife); Fanewraith's Insurgents (scouting ops).
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Withered beast tamers.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Wardens ally with Insurgents against Haven; tamers rival for control.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - Chieftain Drak (Drakona, Ridgeborne, Warrior/Leader): Proud, horned veteran.
    - Serra Horn (Drakona, Ridgeborne, Ranger/Shaman): Visionary, grieving mutated herds.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Chieftain Drak: Climbing routes to Nikta sightings. Reveals via hunt quest.
    - Serra: Rohkin's beast lore. Shares after tribute.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Hidden in crag caves; tolerated by anti-Haven clans.
- **Rebel Activity:** Scouting Haven; recruiting climbers. Viewed as strategic allies.
- **Information Gathering:** Thorn carvings; wind whispers; scout reports.

### Nikta Connection/ Link to the Greater Struggles

- **Place in the World:** Eastern Fanewick crags, a high vantage in the eye hunt and invasion.
