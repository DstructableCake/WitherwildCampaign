### **Location Tier:** Tier 4

### Overview / Description

- **General Description:** A massive tree-hollow nest for avian divinities, woven with thorny vines and enlarged by Witherwild.
- **Sensory Details:** Chirping echoes and feather rustles; scent of fresh eggs and sap; windy heights with vertigo-inducing drops.
- **Architecture & Layout:** Colossal tree with nested levels; vine bridges and feather-lined chambers.
- **Notable Features:** Giant eggs; Withered avian hybrids; shrines to Rohkin.
- **Atmosphere:** Whimsical and heroic, a lofty sanctuary turned predatory fortress.

### Recent and Mid-Term History

- **Recent History (Last 1-5 years):** Witherwild enlarged birds, displacing worshippers; rebels use it as a base near Nikta.
- **Mid-Term History (Past Decades):** Sacred site for wildborne, with traditions of egg tributes.
- **Key Historical Events:** The "Nest Awakening" (1 year ago), where corruption turned guardians hostile.

### Population and Demographics

- **Population Size:** Small (100-150 in treetop villages).
- **Ancestries:** Fauns and firbolgs (horns mimicking beaks from Witherwild).
- **Communities:** Wildborne (divinity guardians).
- **Age Profile:** Elder-heavy, with youth trained as acolytes.
- **Classes & Occupations:** Druids and rangers as wardens.
- **Faint Divinity Affiliations:** Rohkin venerated with herd tributes; customs include "flight rituals."

### Hierarchy & Governance

- **Formal Structure:** High Priestess leading divinity worship.
- **Informal Power:** Avian shamans interpreting omens.
- **Power Dynamics:** Tensions between purists and those embracing corruption.

### Factions & Groups

- **Dominant Factions:** Nikta's Wardens (protecting the nest); Fanewraith's Insurgents (allied for Nikta access).
- **Minor Factions/Undercurrents:** Withered Covenant hybrids.
- **Inter-Faction Relations:** Wardens ally with Insurgents against Haven; Covenant rivals all.

### Key NPCs & Information Providers

- **List of Key NPCs:**
    - High Priestess Elara (Firbolg, Wildborne, Druid/Leader): Devout, conflicted by corruption.
    - Rook the Hybrid (Faun, Wildborne, Ranger/Guardian): Half-Withered, seeking redemption.
- **Information They Possess:**
    - Elara: Nikta's weft access rituals. Reveals via oath or quest.
    - Rook: Corruption cures and rebel plans. Shares if helped.

### Fanewraith's Connection / Rebel Activity

- **Fanewraith's Presence:** Open base in upper nests; protected by Wardens.
- **Rebel Activity:** Planning Sowing Eye theft; training avians. Seen as saviors.
- **Information Gathering:** Feather messages; overheard rituals; hybrid witnesses.

### Potential Quest Hooks [2-3 suggested minimum]

- **Hook 1: Egg of Destiny**
    - **Hook Discovery Methods:** Omen from Rohkin; Elara's plea.
    - **Quest Style:** Zelda heroic: Protect a sacred egg from corruption.
    - **Quest Summary:** Safeguard Nikta-linked egg.
    - **Milestone Markers:** Defend nest; purify egg; hatch ritual.
    - **Mechanics Focus:** Combat (avian battles), puzzles (vine navigation), Wither tokens from corrupted foes.
    - **Impacts:**
        - **Relationships:** Strengthens ties with Wardens; potential alliance with Insurgents if shared.
        - **Environment:** Purifies a nest section, reducing aerial hazards.
        - **Social Standing:** Revered as "Nest Saviors" among wildborne.
        - **Rewards:** Avian familiar (scouting ally), Rohkin's boon (flight-related ability), lore on Nikta's weft.
- **Hook 2: Hybrid Redemption**
    - **Hook Discovery Methods:** Random encounter with Rook; exploration of lower nests.
    - **Quest Style:** Witcher moral complexity: Redeem or condemn Withered hybrids, exploring transformation and grief.
    - **Quest Summary:** Help Rook cure his corruption or integrate it, uncovering Fanewraith's influence.
    - **Milestone Markers:** Gather rare herbs; confront inner demons (Fear Die ritual); decide fate.
    - **Mechanics Focus:** Social rolls (empathy), exploration, downtime (ritual crafting).
    - **Impacts:**
        - **Relationships:** Improves with Covenant if redeemed; strains Insurgents if betrayal occurs.
        - **Environment:** Stabilizes hybrids, creating neutral guardians.
        - **Social Standing:** Seen as compassionate healers or ruthless purgers.
        - **Rewards:** Hybrid artifact (grants temporary Wither resistance), Rook as contact, favor from Rohkin.
- **Hook 3: Weft Intrusion**
    - **Hook Discovery Methods:** Overheard ritual; Faint Divinity vision from Elara.
    - **Quest Style:** BG3 epic intrigue: Enter the spirit weft to spy on Nikta, choosing to aid or thwart Fanewraith.
    - **Quest Summary:** Infiltrate Nikta's realm to prevent Sowing Eye theft.
    - **Milestone Markers:** Perform entry ritual; navigate weft illusions; confront rebels.
    - **Mechanics Focus:** Investigation, puzzles (weft mazes), optional combat.
    - **Impacts:**
        - **Relationships:** Shifts allegiance with Wardens or Insurgents based on choice.
        - **Environment:** Alters nest's spiritual aura, possibly summoning guardians.
        - **Social Standing:** Legendary as weft-walkers.
        - **Rewards:** Weft shard (portal item), Nikta's vision (clue to Reaping Eye), faction favor.
