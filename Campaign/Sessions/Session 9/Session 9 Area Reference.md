## Tree 1 - The Council Oak (Sun-Lofts)

### 1A - The Skyward Archive

**Tree & Layer**: Tree 1 - The Council Oak (Sun-Lofts, 200-300ft)

**Sensory Description**:
- **Sight**: Towering shelves of ancient tomes, scrolls cascading from ceiling hooks, maps pinned to bark walls, soft glow from bioluminescent reading lamps, dust motes dancing in sunbeams filtering through leaf-windows, Elder Corin's scholarly robes moving between stacks
- **Sound**: Quiet rustle of turning pages, whispered scholarly debates, creak of wooden ladders, distant chimes marking study hours, hushed conversations in alcoves
- **Smell**: Old parchment, ink, dried herbs used for preservation, faint scent of night blooms from pressed flowers in books, musty wisdom
- **Touch**: Smooth, worn wooden shelves, rough bark walls, cool air at this height, soft rugs underfoot, weight of ancient knowledge
- **Taste**: Dusty air, faint metallic tang from old ink

**What Players Find**: Ancient texts, maps of Fanewick, records of Thorngrowth Incident, information about Faint Divinities, Theodore's research notes, council historical documents

**Key NPCs**:
- **Lorekeeper Aelric** (Elf, conflicted scholar) - Archive keeper, knows secrets, torn between loyalty and truth
- **Elder Corin** (Human Loreborne, secret Fanewraith agent) - Charismatic scholar, secretly feeds information to Evelyn

**Interactions**: Research checks reveal secrets about Fanewraith, Theodore's background, Brindle's history. Restricted access - requires council permission or scholar credentials.

**Plot Connections**: Elder Corin works here, may reveal information or betray PCs; Aelric knows about Theodore's research. Key location for discovering historical context and potential evidence of Theodore's innocence.

**Access Requirements**: Restricted - Elder Council members and approved scholars only. May require social checks or connections to gain entry.
