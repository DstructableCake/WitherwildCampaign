## Custom Adversary Stats (Referenced Above)

### Haven Patroller (Tier 1 Standard)
- **Difficulty:** 11 | **Thresholds:** 4/8 | **HP:** 4 | **Stress:** 2
- **ATK:** +2 | **Halberd:** Close | 1d6+2 physical or **Crossbow:** Far | 1d8+1 physical
- **Features:**
  - **Hold the Line - Action:** Allies within Close gain +1 Defense until start of your next turn
  - **Rally - Reaction:** When an ally drops, you gain +1 to your next attack this scene

### Fanewraith Skulker (Tier 1 Skulk)
- **Difficulty:** 12 | **Thresholds:** 5/9 | **HP:** 4 | **Stress:** 3
- **ATK:** +2 | **Withered Blade:** Close | 1d6+2 physical
- **Features:**
  - **Shadow Strike - Action:** If target is not in bright light, mark a Stress to deal 1d8+3
  - **Fade Away - Reaction:** When targeted, teleport 20 ft into shadow and gain advantage on next attack

### Root-Whip Hulk (Tier 2 Bruiser)
- **Difficulty:** 13 | **Thresholds:** 7/13 | **HP:** 7 | **Stress:** 3
- **ATK:** +2 | **Thorn Lash:** Close | 1d6+2 wither
- **Features:**
  - **Uproot - Action:** Tear up a 10-ft patch (difficult terrain; creatures entering must save Instinct or become Hesitant until they clear 1 HP)
  - **Sap Surge - Reaction:** When hit by fire, lash the nearest creature for 1d4 wither

### Wither-Stampede (Tier 2 Horde)
- **Difficulty:** 12 | **Thresholds:** 6/10 | **HP:** 6 | **Stress:** 2
- **ATK:** +1 | **Trample:** Close | 1d6+1 physical; on hit, target must save Agility or be knocked Prone
- **Features:**
  - **Flow Around - Action:** Move through a 15-ft line; creatures in line test Instinct or mark 1 Stress
  - **Break and Run - Reaction:** When reduced to half HP, scatter; next round, re-form at a different lane

### Clank Harvester (Tier 2 Hazard)
- **Difficulty:** 12 | **Thresholds:** 6/10 | **HP:** 6 | **Stress:** 0
- **ATK:** +0 | **Sweep Arm:** Close | 1d6 physical to objects/creatures in 10-ft arc
- **Features:**
  - **Jam - Action:** On a failure by a helper, arm sticks open; patrollers may try to wrench it free (Strength 12)
  - **Kill Switch - Action:** If deactivated (Knowledge 13), Clank Lane gains +1 boon to pathing for the round

## GM Notes

### Encounter Selection Guidelines:
- **Start with Cluster 1** for a manageable opening
- **Use Cluster 2** for standard pressure
- **Use Cluster 3** for high-stakes moments
- **Use Cluster 4** only if the party is handling everything well
- **Use Cluster 5** for intelligence-focused encounters
- **Use Cluster 6** for environmental-heavy encounters

### Tactical Considerations:
- **Lane Management:** Each encounter is designed to pressure different lanes
- **Escalation:** Later clusters increase in complexity and threat
- **Environmental Hazards:** Clank Harvesters add tactical complexity
- **Coordination:** Some clusters feature leaders that coordinate attacks
- **Flexibility:** Mix and match elements from different clusters as needed

### Fear Token Usage:
- **Low Fear (1-2):** Use basic adversary features
- **Medium Fear (3-4):** Use Fear features and coordinated attacks
- **High Fear (5+):** Use multiple Fear features and create dramatic moments

### Victory Conditions:
- **Primary:** Save civilians and minimize field loss
- **Secondary:** Identify Fanewraith's patterns and timing
- **Tertiary:** Disrupt the assault and force retreat

This system provides flexible, balanced encounters that can adapt to the party's performance and the dramatic needs of the scene.
